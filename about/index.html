About


Wildcard Robotics 4646 is a robotics team based in Polk City, Iowa.
Wildcard consists of two schools, North Polk High School and Ankeny Centennial High School.
Wildcard started in 2021, taking over Team ASAP. View our history here
Wildcard meets at least once a week in Polk City. To join Wildcard, contact one of our adult volunteers.
